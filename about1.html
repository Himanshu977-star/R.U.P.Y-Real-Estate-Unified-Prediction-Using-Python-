About Krishival

Our platform is designed to empower farmers with data-driven insights to make informed decisions about crop selection, pricing, and disease management. By leveraging AI, real-time data, and market analysis, we provide farmers with tools that improve productivity, profitability, and sustainability.

What We Provide & How It Helps Farmers

🌱 Crop Recommendations

What It Does: Suggests the best crops to grow based on weather conditions, soil quality, and market demand.

How It Helps Farmers: Prevents crop failure due to unsuitable weather and ensures maximum yield & profit.

📊 Live Crops Graph

What It Does: Displays real-time data on which crops farmers are planning to grow.

How It Helps Farmers: Prevents overproduction, helps farmers choose less-grown crops for better pricing, and balances market demand.

🦠🌾 Crop Disease Prediction

What It Does: Farmers can upload images of their crops, and our AI detects diseases and suggests treatments.

How It Helps Farmers: Early detection prevents major losses, reduces the use of harmful pesticides, and ensures a healthier yield.

💰 Crop Price Prediction

What It Does: Uses historical data, supply-demand analysis, and market trends to forecast crop prices.

How It Helps Farmers: Farmers can time their sales for maximum profit and avoid losses due to market fluctuations.

☁️🌧️ Weather-Based Insights

What It Does: Provides real-time weather forecasts and suggests crops that suit current climatic conditions.

How It Helps Farmers: Avoids losses due to unpredictable weather and ensures seasonal crop planning.

🏛️ Government Schemes & Support

What It Does: Lists government subsidies, loans, and financial aid programs available for farmers.

How It Helps Farmers: Farmers can apply for financial benefits directly through the platform and improve their agricultural investments.

📉 Smart Farming Insights (Data-Driven Farming)

What It Does: Uses AI and data analytics to provide smart insights for better farming decisions.

How It Helps Farmers: Helps in precision farming, reducing resource wastage, and increasing crop quality & yield.

Our mission is to empower farmers with technology, prevent financial losses, and promote sustainable agriculture for a better future.
