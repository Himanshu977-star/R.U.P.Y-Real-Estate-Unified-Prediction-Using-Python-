Frequently Asked Questions

What is this platform about?
This platform helps farmers plan their crops by providing real-time insights on crop trends, price predictions, weather suitability, and disease detection to improve productivity and income.
Is this platform free for farmers?
Yes! The platform is completely free for all farmers, ensuring easy access to valuable agricultural insights.
Does the platform provide information about government schemes?
Yes, it lists various government schemes and subsidies, helping farmers apply for financial support and benefits.
How does the live crops graph benefit farmers?
The Live Crops Graph displays real-time data on which crops farmers are planning to grow. This helps prevent overproduction and ensures better price stability in the market.
What is crop disease prediction, and how does it work?
The AI-powered system allows farmers to upload images of their crops, detects potential diseases, and provides recommendations for treatment and prevention.
How accurate is the crop disease prediction system?
The AI model is trained on thousands of disease samples and provides highly accurate results with proper treatment recommendations.
Does this platform support multiple languages?
Yes! The platform is available in multiple regional languages to ensure accessibility for all farmers.
How does the platform help prevent crop wastage?
By analyzing real-time crop data, it warns farmers about overproduction risks and suggests alternative crops to reduce wastage.
